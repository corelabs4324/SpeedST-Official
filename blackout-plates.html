Questions

Is this legal on the road?

Plate visibility laws apply on public roads in every state. Blackout plates are sold for show, off-road and private-property use — you're responsible for when and where you activate it.

Will it fit my plate?

Standard (372 × 134 mm) covers most AU rear plates; slimline (372 × 100 mm) suits compact front plates. The dual pack can mix sizes.

How is it powered?

USB-C with included 12V adapter and extension leads. Hardwire kit available for clean installs.

What's the warranty?

12 months on the film, controller and remote. Replacement parts ship from our AU warehouse.
