Questions

Matte or gloss?

Matte black reads stealth and hides on dark paint. Gloss is high contrast and pops on any panel or glass.

How do I apply the banner?

The banner ships pre-masked. Clean the glass, position with the supplied guide tabs, squeegee, peel the mask. Ten minutes solo.

Will they come off cleanly?

Yes — heat the vinyl with a hair dryer and peel slowly. Any residue lifts with isopropyl.
