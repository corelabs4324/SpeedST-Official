Questions

V1 or Pro?

V1 gives you 16M solid colours, fades and strobes from an RF remote. Pro adds addressable LEDs for chase animations, app control and music sync. Both use the same housings and loom.

Is underglow legal?

Rules vary by state — most restrict certain colours and flashing modes on public roads. Underglow is sold for show and private-property use; check your local rules.

How hard is the install?

Bars mount with supplied brackets or 3M VHB. The loom runs to your battery with an inline fuse. Most installs take about an hour with basic tools.

What if a bar fails?

12-month warranty. Individual bars are replaceable — you won't bin the whole kit.
